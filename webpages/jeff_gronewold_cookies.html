Ingredients

  • 15 tb unsalted butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 Tbs vanilla
  • 1.5 cups white sugar
  • 1 Tbs brown sugar
  • 2 Tbs maple syrup
  • 2.5 cups flower
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Cookies

Description

These cookies are pretty much the best ones in the world.

Directions

  1. Melt butter in either the microwave or in a steel bowl in the over
  2. Preheat oven to 325
  3. Allow butter to mostly cool to room temperature, add sugars, syrup, and vanilla
  4. Crack the eggs, one at a time, into sugar/butter mixture. Beat on low until well mixed
  5. In a separate bowl, combine all the dry ingredients
  6. Slowly mix in the dry ingredients to the sugar/butter mixture, beating until well mixed
  7. Add choclate chips to the dough!
  8. Refridgerate or freeze dough for at least two hours to prepare the dough
  9. Roll dough into Tbs size balls and bake for 8-10 minutes or until golden brown around the edges
